Hello there, fellow internaut!

You might well be wondering to whom this digital abode belongs.
Fear not, I shall enlighten you! My name is Moritz. I love traveling and programming. Currently I am combining both; living in Japan for a year on a working-holiday visa. I get to travel around, working on various side-projects when I have had enough sightseeing. Programming is my passion and, back home, where I am finishing a master in computer science and business, it is also my day-job.

Learning new things holds a strange fascination for me, as does discovering new places and experiencing different cultures. Hence, I enjoy learning new programming languages (especially functional ones), concepts, technologies and traveling.

What is this then?
This is my personal blog. Here you can read my ramblings, musings and write-ups (aka. tutorials) on technical and not-so-technical topics.

If you want to connect, you can find me on google+ and github.
